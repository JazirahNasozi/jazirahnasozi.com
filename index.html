Joining the CodeQueen program was a turning point in my journey. Through this program, I deepened my understanding of coding, problem-solving, and digital creativity. These experiences, alongside my learning in programs like CodeQueen, have shaped my journey into one defined by resilience, curiosity, and a passion for making a positive impact through technology.
